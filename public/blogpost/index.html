The Tackle Shop: Embedded Checkout for Game Publishers

How FastSpring's SBL and Embedded Checkout help game publishers monetize directly, no backend required.

Are you a game publisher looking to monetize your game offerings? We get it. App stores are starting to take a bite out of your revenue. Tracking subscriptions is a headache you don't need. With FastSpring, your webapp or mobile game gets a checkout experience that just works. Embed it directly into your application or site to keep players in the flow, and close more conversions with a branded experience they trust.

FastSpring provides tools to process payments while reducing what you need to build.

With FastSpring, your teams can:

  • Get real-time product catalogs: synced prices, product descriptions, and images from one dashboard. Update currencies for new markets without writing a line of code.
  • Have subscriptions handled: activate, track, and manage recurring revenue from one central place. No backend required.
  • See orders and analytics: every purchase, every subscription event, tracked and accessible in one platform.

Is your team growing internationally? FastSpring handles localized pricing, taxes, and currencies out of the box. That means more time testing new markets and less engineering time.

Your mobile site is the gateway to all the in-app fun. The Store Builder Library and embedded checkout give you the building blocks to get to market fast, monetize from day one, and simplify the customer experience. All you have to do is Embed. Test. Iterate. Focus on building and let FastSpring take care of the rest.

Future Build Proposal: Account Management API

If given more time, the next integration would be FastSpring's Account Management API. This would allow players to manage their own subscriptions: upgrade, downgrade, cancel, and update payment methods, directly from within the game UI, without redirecting to an external portal. For a game publisher scaling subscription offerings across international markets, self-service account management reduces support ticket volume, lowers churn by removing friction from plan changes, and keeps players inside the branded experience. Combined with the webhook infrastructure already in place, this turns the POC from a checkout demo into a full customer lifecycle platform, exactly what a monetization team needs to operate at scale.
